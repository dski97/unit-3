Fun Facts about Arizona


  • Arizona is the 48th state in the United States.
  • It is the 6th largest state by area.
  • It is the 14th most populous state.
  • The capital of Arizona is Phoenix.
  • Arizona has the largest percentage of land designated as Indian tribal land in the United States.
  • The world's largest solar telescope, the McMath-Pierce Solar Telescope, is located in Arizona.
  • Arizona is home to the Grand Canyon, one of the world's seven natural wonders.
  • The state's official flower is the saguaro cactus blossom.
